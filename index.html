About Me

PhD in Machine Learning for Healthcare

Hey, I'm Bowen. I received my PhD from ETH Zurich under the supervision of Prof. Karsten Borgwardt, who is now a director at MPI of Biochemistry. During my PhD, I was also a student of the ELLIS Society and a fellow of the MLFPM Marie Curie Innovative Training Network. My research focuses on developing machine learning algorithms to address a variety of topics in the biomedical domain. For more details of my research projects, please refer to the Research section.

Prior to that, I received my master's degree from the University of Tokyo under the supervision of Prof. Ichiro Sakuma, and my master thesis was honored with the Dean's Choice Award for Best Thesis from the Department of Precision Engineering (Department of Precision Engineering). I earned my bachelor's degree from Tsinghua University.

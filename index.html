About Me

PhD in Machine Learning for Healthcare

Hey, I'm Bowen. I received my PhD from ETH Zurich under the supervision of Prof. Karsten Borgwardt, who is now a director at MPI of Biochemistry. I am also a doctoral student of the ELLIS Society and a fellow of the MLFPM Marie Curie Innovative Training Network. I am very passionate about using machine learning approaches to address a variety of topics in the biomedical domain, check here to know more about my research.

Prior to that, I received my master's degree from the University of Tokyo under the supervision of Prof. Ichiro Sakuma, and my master thesis was honored with the Dean's Choice Award for Best Thesis from the (Department of Precision Engineering). I earned my bachelor's degree from Tsinghua University.

Apart from research, I am an avid sports enthusiast. I cherish the time spent engaging in various sports like basketball, CrossFit, and bouldering. These activities energize me and bring a profound sense of courage and passion into my life.
